Stay Safe Online – Prevention Tips

1. Never share your OTP or passwords with anyone.
2. Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts.
3. Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible.
4. Always check website URLs before entering information.
5. Avoid clicking on suspicious links or unknown emails.
6. Do not use public WiFi for online banking transactions.
7. Use only official apps for payments and services.
8. Regularly update your software and antivirus.

Report cybercrime at: cybercrime.gov.in
