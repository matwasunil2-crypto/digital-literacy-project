Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

Do’s:
1. Use strong and secure passwords.
2. Share useful and positive content.
3. Be respectful while communicating online.
4. Keep your profile professional.
5. Verify information before sharing.

Don’ts:
1. Do not share personal or sensitive information.
2. Do not click on unknown or suspicious links.
3. Do not post offensive or harmful content.
4. Do not spread fake news or misinformation.
5. Do not overshare your private life online.
